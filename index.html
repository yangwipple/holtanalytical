What is Mold?

Molds are microscopic organisms found virtually everywhere, indoors and outdoors.

Most people think that mold is slimy, blackish-green discolorations that gradually increase in size, but found only in dirty, unkempt homes or apartments.

The truth is, mold and spores can flourish in sparkling clean environments as well.
